Tsundoko
This site was both a way for me to show my love of literature and work with more javascript. It is a straightforward collection of book reviews designed to look like a library or pile of books. Using jquery each book cover changes to a more legible form and links to the review itself. The bigger challenge came from the recommendations page. Wanting to practice more javascript, I wrote a randomizer for book recommendations. The reader selects which genre they'd like a recommnedation for and the page loads a book I have read in that genre. All the books included are books I have read and enjoyed, though not all have a written review. Those that have written reviews link to the review and those that don't link to website where more information on the book can be obtained.
Hopping The Pond
A website designed around the idea of polaroid snapshots. My wife and I began blogging about our experiences abroad and this was the perfect opportunity to develop my own website. The site was designed to be simple and straight forward. Polaroid pictures of each topic link to articles. For those searching for specific location, a collapseable list organized by country was added. The page utilizes HTML/CSS as well as a bit of jquery for both the link highlights and the collapseable country list. CSS was used to design the polaroid look of each link.
